Student Perks

Being a student we are loaded with stress from exams and submissions etc. However, there are also positives of being a student. There are many student perks which ranges from cheaper priced food in school canteens to discounted products. Such organisations offer these discounts and perks to help aid students financially so that they will be able to unlock their potential. Here are some student perks offered to students in Singapore!:



  • Transport
  • Apple
  • Razer

Diploma Student Concession Card

With the Diploma Student Concession Card, you can enjoy student fares and purchase a Monthly Concession Pass (MCP) for unlimited travel on train and/or basic bus services (trunk and feeder services).

Eligibility Criteria

You must be a full-time matriculated Diploma student* from the following nine government/government-aided tertiary institutions:
  • BCA Academy
  • ITE (Diploma)
  • LaSalle College of the Arts
  • Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts (NAFA)
  • Nanyang Polytechnic
  • Ngee Ann Polytechnic
  • Republic Polytechnic
  • Singapore Polytechnic
  • Temasek Polytechnic
