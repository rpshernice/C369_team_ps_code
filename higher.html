Higher Year Scholarship

In partnership with industry partners and organisations, Higher Year Scholarships are awarded to outstanding RP students.


Eligibility

  • Polytechnic Foundation Programme graduates who are entering Year 1; and Diploma students who are in their Year 2 and Year 3
  • Excellent academic results
  • Active involvement in non-academic related activities e.g. in CCAs, community projects, leadership programmes
  • Good character and conduct
  • Not a recipient of another scholarship
  • Other eligibility criteria specified by the sponsor may apply
  • Other eligibility criteria specified by the sponsor may apply Successful recipients may be offered opportunities to serve an internship with the sponsor


    • Application Process


    Application for Higher Year Scholarships is by invitation only. Shortlisted students will be invited for an interview by the Selection Panel.
