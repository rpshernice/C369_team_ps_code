External Scholarship

Republic Polytechnic (RP) students can consider applying for scholarships administered by various sponsoring organisations. The list of external scholarships is not limited to the scholarships below and the information from the sponsoring organisations may change from time to time.
Students who are interested in these scholarships are advised to check the sponsors' websites or external scholarship portals (refer to the section below for details) for the most updated information and check their RP email account regularly for updates on the latest scholarships.
